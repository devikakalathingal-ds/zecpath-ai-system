Job Summary

marketing surveillance.

• Evaluate risk-benefit profiles of drugs
• Support approval and post-marketing decisions
• Collaborate with expert committees
• Monitor ongoing safety data and signals

Qualifications

Skills

• Scientific evaluation
• Regulatory expertise
• Critical thinking
• Decision-making
