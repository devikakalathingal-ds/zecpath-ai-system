Job Summary

Responsible for end-to-end management of Individual Case Safety Reports (ICSRs), ensuring
accuracy, completeness, and compliance with global regulatory requirements.

Key Responsibilities

• Perform case intake, data entry, and validation of ICSRs
• Assess case seriousness, expectedness, and causality (basic to intermediate level)
• Perform medical coding using MedDRA & WHO-DD
• Ensure timely submission to regulatory authorities (FDA, EMA, etc.)
• Conduct follow-ups for missing information
• Maintain case documentation and audit readiness

Qualifications

• B.Pharm / M.Pharm / Life Sciences / PharmD

Skills

• Strong ICSR processing knowledge
• Attention to detail
• Knowledge of global safety regulations
