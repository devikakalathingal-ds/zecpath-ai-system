Job Summary

A medically qualified professional responsible for evaluating drug safety data and making
clinical decisions regarding patient safety.

Key Responsibilities

• Perform medical review of complex and serious cases
• Provide expert opinion on causality and risk assessment
• Contribute to benefit-risk evaluation
• Support regulatory submissions and safety documents
• Participate in signal detection and risk management
• Interact with regulatory authorities when required

Qualifications

• MBBS / MD (preferred)

Skills

• Advanced clinical expertise
• Decision-making ability
• Regulatory knowledge
• Communication skills
