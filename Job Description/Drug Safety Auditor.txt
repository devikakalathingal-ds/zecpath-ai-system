Job Summary

Performs audits of pharmacovigilance systems and processes to ensure regulatory compliance
and quality standards.

Key Responsibilities

• Plan and conduct PV audits (internal & external)
• Evaluate compliance with global safety regulations
• Prepare audit reports and findings
• Recommend corrective and preventive actions (CAPA)
• Follow up on audit observations
• Ensure audit readiness at all times

Qualifications

• Pharmacy / Life Sciences / QA background

Skills

• Auditing techniques
• Regulatory knowledge
• Analytical and reporting skills
