Job Summary

Responsible for monitoring and evaluating drug safety at a national level to ensure public
health protection.

Key Responsibilities

• Monitor adverse drug reactions (ADRs) across healthcare systems
• Analyze safety data and identify potential risks
• Ensure compliance with national drug safety regulations
• Collaborate with hospitals, pharma companies, and regulators
• Prepare safety reports and recommendations
• Support policy development for drug safety

Qualifications

• B.Pharm / M.Pharm / PharmD / MBBS

Skills

• Regulatory knowledge
• Data analysis
• Public health awareness
• Communication skills
