Job Summary

Acts as a bridge between pharmacovigilance and regulatory affairs, ensuring safety data is
properly submitted to authorities.

Key Responsibilities

• Prepare and submit safety reports to regulatory authorities
• Ensure compliance with submission timelines
• Maintain regulatory documentation and records
• Support product registration and lifecycle management
• Track regulatory changes and update processes
• Coordinate with global regulatory teams

Qualifications

• B.Pharm / M.Pharm / Life Sciences

Skills

• Regulatory submission knowledge
• Documentation and coordination
• Understanding of global regulations
