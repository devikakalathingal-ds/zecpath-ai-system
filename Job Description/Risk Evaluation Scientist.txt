Job Summary

Assesses and quantifies risks associated with pharmaceutical products to support benefit-risk
decisions.

Key Responsibilities

• Conduct risk assessments using safety and clinical data
• Quantify risks and evaluate their impact
• Develop risk minimization strategies
• Support benefit-risk evaluation frameworks
• Contribute to regulatory submissions and safety reports
• Collaborate with safety, clinical, and regulatory teams

Qualifications

• PharmD / M.Pharm / PhD / MBBS

Skills

• Risk assessment and modeling
• Data interpretation
• Decision-making skills
• Regulatory knowledge
