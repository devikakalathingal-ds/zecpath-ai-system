Job Summary

Ensures that all pharmacovigilance (PV) activities comply with global regulatory
requirements and internal policies.

Key Responsibilities

• Monitor compliance with global PV regulations (ICH-GVP, FDA, EMA)
• Conduct internal audits and compliance checks
• Identify compliance gaps and implement corrective actions (CAPA)
• Maintain SOPs and ensure process adherence
• Support regulatory inspections and audits
• Provide compliance training to teams

Qualifications

• B.Pharm / M.Pharm / Life Sciences / PharmD

Skills

• Strong knowledge of PV regulations
• Audit and compliance expertise
• Attention to detail
• Documentation skills
