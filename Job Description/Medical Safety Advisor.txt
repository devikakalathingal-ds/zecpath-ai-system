Job Summary

Provides strategic medical guidance on drug safety and risk management throughout the
product lifecycle.

Key Responsibilities

• Provide medical expertise for safety strategies
• Support risk management and mitigation planning
• Advise on regulatory submissions and labeling
• Review and approve safety-related documents
• Act as a liaison between medical, regulatory, and PV teams
• Support inspections and audits

Qualifications

• MBBS / MD / PharmD

Skills

• Strategic thinking
• Leadership and communication
• Risk assessment expertise
• Regulatory knowledge
