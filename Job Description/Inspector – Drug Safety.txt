Job Summary

Conducts inspections of pharmaceutical companies and healthcare institutions to ensure
compliance with drug safety regulations.

Key Responsibilities

• Inspect pharmacovigilance systems and processes
• Verify compliance with regulatory guidelines
• Identify violations and recommend corrective actions
• Prepare inspection reports
• Support enforcement of drug safety laws

Qualifications

• B.Pharm / M.Pharm / Regulatory background

Skills

• Inspection and auditing
• Regulatory knowledge
• Attention to detail
• Legal awareness
