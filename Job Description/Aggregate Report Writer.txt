Job Summary

Responsible for preparing periodic safety reports that evaluate the overall safety profile of a
drug.

Key Responsibilities

• Prepare aggregate reports such as PSUR, PBRER, DSUR
• Compile and analyze safety data from multiple sources
• Write clinical summaries and risk-benefit evaluations
• Collaborate with medical, regulatory, and safety teams
• Ensure compliance with ICH guidelines and submission timelines
• Perform quality checks and document control

Qualifications

• M.Pharm / PharmD / Life Sciences / Medical background

Skills

• Medical writing
• Data interpretation
• Regulatory knowledge (ICH-GVP)
• Attention to detail
