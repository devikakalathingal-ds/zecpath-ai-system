Job Summary

Monitors and evaluates the safety of vaccines across clinical trials and post-marketing phases
to ensure public health safety.

Key Responsibilities
• Monitor adverse events following immunization (AEFI)
• Analyze vaccine safety data from clinical and real-world sources
• Support benefit-risk evaluation of vaccines
• Collaborate with public health authorities and regulatory agencies
• Prepare safety reports (PSUR, signal reports)
• Investigate safety signals related to vaccines

Qualifications

• PharmD / MBBS / MPH / Life Sciences

Skills

• Knowledge of immunology and vaccines
• Epidemiology and data analysis
• Risk assessment
• Regulatory compliance
