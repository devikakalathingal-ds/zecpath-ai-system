Job Summary

Supports national pharmacovigilance programs like Pharmacovigilance Programme of India
in collecting, analyzing, and reporting safety data.

Key Responsibilities

• Collect and process ADR reports from healthcare providers
• Enter and manage safety data in national databases
• Assist in signal detection and safety evaluation
• Conduct awareness and training programs
• Support reporting to global databases (e.g., WHO Vigibase)

Qualifications

• Pharmacy / Life Sciences / Nursing

Skills

• Data entry and analysis
• Awareness of PV processes
• Training and communication skills
