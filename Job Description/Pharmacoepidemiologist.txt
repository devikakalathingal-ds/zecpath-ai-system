Job Summary

Studies the use and effects of drugs in large populations to understand safety, effectiveness,
and risk factors.

Key Responsibilities

• Design and conduct observational studies (cohort, case-control)
• Analyze population-level healthcare data
• Evaluate drug utilization and safety trends
• Identify risk factors and outcomes
• Publish research findings and reports
• Support regulatory and clinical decision-making

Qualifications

• MPH / PhD / PharmD / Epidemiology / Public Health

Skills

• Epidemiological methods
• Biostatistics and data analysis
• Study design and research methodology
• Scientific writing
