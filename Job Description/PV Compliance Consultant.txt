Job Summary

Ensures pharmacovigilance systems meet global compliance standards and supports
organizations in audit readiness.

Key Responsibilities

• Conduct compliance audits and gap analysis
• Implement CAPA (Corrective and Preventive Actions)
• Ensure adherence to ICH-GVP and regulatory requirements
• Prepare organizations for inspections
• Develop and review SOPs and quality systems
• Provide compliance training

Qualifications

• Pharmacy / Life Sciences / QA background

Skills

• Audit expertise
• Regulatory compliance
• Risk assessment
• Documentation
