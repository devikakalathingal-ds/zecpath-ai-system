Job Summary

Performs clinical review of Individual Case Safety Reports (ICSRs) to ensure medical
accuracy, proper assessment, and regulatory compliance.

Key Responsibilities

• Review adverse event cases for clinical accuracy
• Assess seriousness, causality, and expectedness
• Validate medical narratives and coding
• Provide medical input for case processing and reporting
• Support signal detection and aggregate reporting
• Ensure compliance with global regulatory guidelines

Qualifications

• MBBS / BDS / PharmD

Skills

• Strong clinical knowledge
• Medical judgment & reasoning
• Attention to detail
• Knowledge of MedDRA coding
