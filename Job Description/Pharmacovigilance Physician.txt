Job Summary

Oversees pharmacovigilance activities from a medical perspective, ensuring patient safety
and regulatory compliance.

Key Responsibilities

• Lead medical review of safety data
• Evaluate adverse event reports across products
• Support aggregate reporting (PSUR, PBRER)
• Contribute to risk management plans (RMPs)
• Provide medical oversight to PV teams
• Participate in audits and inspections

Qualifications

• MBBS / MD

Skills

• Pharmacovigilance expertise
• Leadership skills
• Analytical thinking
• Regulatory compliance knowledge
