Job Summary

Ensures safety and regulatory compliance of medical devices through monitoring and
reporting adverse events.

Key Responsibilities

• Monitor adverse events related to medical devices
• Ensure compliance with medical device regulations (EU MDR, FDA)
• Investigate device-related incidents and malfunctions
• Prepare vigilance reports and regulatory submissions
• Collaborate with engineering and quality teams

Qualifications

• Biomedical / Pharmacy / Life Sciences

Skills

• Knowledge of medical device regulations
• Risk assessment
• Technical understanding of devices
• Analytical skills
