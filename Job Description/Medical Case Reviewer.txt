Job Summary

Performs in-depth medical review of ICSRs to ensure clinical accuracy and proper
evaluation.

• Assess seriousness, causality, and expectedness
• Provide medical judgment on adverse events
• Ensure clinical consistency in reports
• Support aggregate reporting and signal detection

Qualifications

Skills

• Strong clinical knowledge
• Medical writing skills
• Critical thinking
