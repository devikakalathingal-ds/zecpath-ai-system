Job Summary

Identifies, evaluates, and validates potential safety signals from large pharmacovigilance
datasets to ensure early detection of drug-related risks.

Key Responsibilities

• Perform signal detection using safety databases (ICSRs, EudraVigilance, FAERS)
• Apply statistical methods (disproportionality analysis, PRR, ROR)
• Validate and prioritize safety signals
• Collaborate with medical and regulatory teams for signal evaluation
• Prepare signal detection reports and documentation
• Support regulatory submissions and safety communications

Qualifications

• M.Pharm / PharmD / PhD / Biostatistics / Life Sciences

Skills

• Strong statistical and analytical skills
• Knowledge of signal detection methodologies
• Data interpretation and problem-solving
• Experience with tools like SAS, R, Python
