Job Summary

Generates insights from real-world data (RWD) to support drug safety, effectiveness, and
regulatory decisions.

• Analyze real-world datasets (EHR, claims data, registries)
• Generate evidence for safety and effectiveness
• Support post-marketing surveillance studies
• Develop RWE reports for regulatory submissions
• Collaborate with cross-functional teams
• Ensure data quality and compliance

Qualifications

• Life Sciences / Data Science / Epidemiology / Pharmacy

Skills

• Data analytics (SQL, Python, R)
• Knowledge of healthcare databases
• Statistical modeling
• Critical thinking
