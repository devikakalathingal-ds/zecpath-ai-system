Job Summary

Combines clinical knowledge with scientific analysis to evaluate safety data from clinical
trials and post-marketing sources.

Key Responsibilities

• Analyze safety data from clinical studies
• Identify trends and potential safety signals
• Support benefit-risk assessment
• Contribute to safety sections of clinical study reports
• Collaborate with clinical and regulatory teams
• Prepare safety documentation

Qualifications

• PharmD / M.Pharm / PhD / MBBS

Skills

• Clinical data interpretation
• Scientific writing
• Analytical skills
• Understanding of clinical trials
