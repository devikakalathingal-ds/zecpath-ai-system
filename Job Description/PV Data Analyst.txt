Job Summary

Analyzes pharmacovigilance data to generate insights, identify trends, and support safety
decision-making.

Key Responsibilities

• Extract and analyze safety data from PV databases
• Identify trends, patterns, and anomalies in adverse event data
• Create dashboards and reports for stakeholders
• Support signal detection and risk assessment
• Perform data cleaning and validation
• Collaborate with safety and regulatory teams

Qualifications

• Life Sciences / Pharmacy / Data Science / Biostatistics

Skills

• Data analysis (Excel, SQL, Python, R)
• Visualization tools (Power BI, Tableau)
• Statistical knowledge
• Analytical thinking
