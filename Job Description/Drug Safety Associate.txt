Job Summary

A Drug Safety Associate is responsible for collecting, processing, and reporting adverse
event data to ensure patient safety and regulatory compliance.

Key Responsibilities

• Process Individual Case Safety Reports (ICSRs)
• Perform data entry into safety databases (e.g., Argus, ArisG)
• Conduct case triage and prioritization
• Perform medical coding using MedDRA and WHO-DD
• Ensure compliance with global regulations (ICH, FDA, EMA)
• Follow up on missing or incomplete safety data
• Maintain accurate documentation

Qualifications

• B.Pharm / M.Pharm / Life Sciences / Nursing / MBBS

Skills

• Attention to detail
• Basic medical knowledge
• Data handling & documentation
• Good communication skills
