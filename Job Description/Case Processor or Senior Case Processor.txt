Job Summary

Handles processing of safety cases with higher accuracy and complexity; senior roles may
mentor juniors and review cases.

Key Responsibilities

• Process adverse event cases from multiple sources
• Perform detailed data validation and quality checks
• Handle complex cases (serious, expedited reporting)
• Ensure compliance with timelines (SLA)
• Review junior team members’ work (Senior role)
• Assist in training and process improvements

Qualifications

• Pharmacy / Life Sciences

Skills

• Case processing expertise
• Time management
• Leadership (for Senior roles)
