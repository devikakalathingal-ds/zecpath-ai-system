Job Summary

Performs pharmacovigilance activities within a CRO environment, handling safety cases for
multiple clients and ensuring compliance with global regulations.

Key Responsibilities

• Process Individual Case Safety Reports (ICSRs) for different clients
• Perform data entry, validation, and medical coding
• Ensure compliance with client-specific and global regulatory requirements
• Meet strict timelines (SLA-driven environment)
• Communicate with clients for case follow-ups
• Maintain documentation and audit readiness

Qualifications

• B.Pharm / M.Pharm / Life Sciences / Nursing

Skills

• ICSR processing
• Time management (high workload handling)
• Client communication
• Attention to detail
